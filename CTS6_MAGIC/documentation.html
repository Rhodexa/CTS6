About CTS6
  AVR Chiptune Studio is a fast wavetable based polyphonic synthesizer designed for ATMEGA328P and its family. It provides simple yet versatile dynamic sound for many AVR projects. Two new version are comming for Blue Pill (STM32F103C8T6) and ESP8266.

News & Features (v6.1)
  · 6 independent operators are provided
  · FM mode is now available!
  · Stereo is now possible!
  · Each operator have its own envelope generator
  · global Vibrato & Tremolo is provided
  · Glide, and Swipe were added! Go funky!
  · As fast as we can, letting time free for your code!
  · Many compiler flags available, making CTS fit your needs
  · Only Timer2 is used, so you have Timer1 and Timer0 all for your self. Interestingly, Timer2 is used to handle Arduino's tone() function, but you won't be using that anymore, since you now have CTS!
  · Pre-made wavetables are now written in program memory, freeing RAM space.
  · Custom wavetables can be easily loaded from outside the library, via
#define ADD_CUSTOM_WAVETABLES


Details
  A total of 6 (0 ··· 5) operators are available, these can be either used in additive mode allowing 6 independent voices, or in pairs getting 3 FM capable voices. FM and Additive modes can be independently selected for each pair, so you can use 4 simple voices and 1 FM (5 total voices), or 2 FM and 2 simple (4 total), etc.

  Each voice have two 8-bit attenuators connected to them, each attenuator is directed to each channel accumulator (L and R channels). Effectively allowing stereo capabilities. You can use
CTS::setStereoLevels(voice, channel_l, channel_r);
to set these attenuators' values.
